The Basic Things
Toram Online is designed for Andriod and IOS smartphones, so trying to play it on an emulator on a PC with a mouse will not be favorable to you at all. Combat AND movement would be difficult.
Toram Online is an immersive 3rd person (non-isometric) and requires both hands to perform combat effectively. If you like playing with one hand, Toram may not be the game for you.
You can use Emulators as a workaround(which will get you banned if you are found out), but since Asobimo Games Inc. is primarily focused on mobile gaming, we won't be getting an official PC port.
People use emulators to use scripts and bots to cheat in the game, so Asobimo bans those who use emulators.
This website or owner is not responsible for any mishaps that occur if you choose to use Emulators.
Toram Online DOES NOT HAVE PVP as of the latest date mentioned above. It will however, be released this year. If you want PVP content then you might have to search for another game.

The Tutorial
The tutorial makes it clear that your character doesn't have any specific lines. All lines are said by NPCs. The NPC lines are based on your most probable real-life response. There are some instances in the story where you can choose different options, but trust me, none of them affect the story's progress or direction. Just some lore if you are interested. Don't be afraid of being rude to the NPCs or whatever. Toram Online tutorial says that Combat is automated. This is only partially true. Tapping the action button once selects the nearest mob and switches the camera to it. Tapping it again makes your character continuously Normal attack it until it dies, after that, your character sheathes your weapon. The normal attacks are the only things automated in combat. You cannot automatically switch to a different mob. You either tap the action button to select the nearest mob or if you want to fight a specific faraway mob, tap on that mob. You can hold action button to see a list of all mobs and players on the map. Normal attacks deal pathetically low amounts of damage once you reach level 50 or above. (Even at level 25-30 they are weak). The main damage dealing concept in Toram Online are 'SKILLS', specifically attacking skills. You'll figure how to get and unlock and upgrade them on your own. Those are NOT AUTOMATED and require you to focus to use skills at the right time in the right order or to know when to stop using skills and dodge. Therefore, COMBAT IN TORAM ONLINE IS NOT AUTOMATED AND REQUIRES A LOT OF WORK. If you favor automated combat, Toram is not for you. Wondering what happens when you kill the dragon in the tutorial?
It takes a long long long time to kill the dragon and you get a 'Proof of Resistance ring.' It is practically useless and is a symbol of wasting your time before the game even begins. Want to Know more about Toram Online's story mode? Click here.

Characters
Characters are separate experiences within the same account.
All your items, your username, achievements, emblems, Premium currency, and avatars(cosmetics) are shared between all the characters.
The characters don't share levels.
More info on levels in 'Levels' section below.
If you want to start fresh, instead of creating a new account, create a new character. That keeps any valuable items you have with you.

There are a lot of things not mentioned in Toram's tutorial that makes your experience of Toram much much easier. Here are some of them.
Remember when I said that selecting a mob causes the camera to lock onto it? If you don't like this, you can change it using
「Menu」➡「Settings」➡「System」➡「Autofocus」➡「Off」.
If you want to run faster, you can go to
「Menu」➡「Settings」➡「System」➡「Dash」➡「On」.
If you don't like the annoying numbers that always pop up when you deal damage, go to
「Menu」➡「Settings」➡「Graphics」➡「Damage Font Size」➡「(change slider)」.
If you don't like the annoying spammers spamming in the 'SHOUT' section of the chat
「Menu」➡「Settings」➡「Chat」➡「Chat Filtering Settings」➡「Toggle Shout」.
If your phone can't handle Graphics, go to
「Menu」➡「Settings」➡「Graphics」and check out the settings.
You can fiddle around with a lot of settings here, it would be really useful.
MENU -> Map -> World map are also helpful. The update in June 2021 has made it even easier to search and teleport to places.

In「Menu」➡「Settings」➡「System」➡「Shortcuts」, the different sets are literally different sets of shortcuts, for different situations. You cannot access a shortcut from set 2 if set 1 is enabled. Inventory
You access your inventory by 「Menu」➡「Items」. Unlike most games, equipping a weapon/armor doesn't vanish it from the inventory. There are 50 inventory spaces, each can hold 99 items. A group of 99 items is called a stack. Weapons and armor and additional gear and ring are non-stackable. You have a storage space, similar to a 'bank locker'. You can access it at any NPC store that has that feature. Usually in Sofya City, El Scaro, and Hora Diomedea. (Unfamiliar names, yes, but soon they will be familiar.) This also has storage space. For increasing storage space in bank you can use orbs. One orb increases one storage space in personal inventory. (which you can move around) One orb opens one inventory slot, but one orb opens 5 bank slots. Choose carefully. When in your inventory, tap an item. On the left are options on what to do with that item. Tap the「1/2」 icon to open up more options on what to do with that item. Stats
Early game, monsters are fairly easy, so it is recommended to add all stat points to boost ATK/MATK. For now, you don't have to worry about VIT stat. Defense, MaxHp, and MaxMP can be gained from equipments.
HIT and FLEE are useless, as of given date above. The game is very unbalanced with respect to those. Monsters have unreasonably high HIT and FLEE compared to players. Don't try to increase these. 'But how can I not miss if I don't increase Accuracy?' Magic never misses, but can be guarded. It also cannot be evaded. Criticals never miss either, but their damage fluctuates a lot if they graze. Levels
The current level cap is 230 as of the given above date.
Toram Online is a 2015 game, so obviously, most players are high level and most events are aimed at level 150+ players. But, there are also events for all levels. You need not worry. Money, (currency called spina) is currently earned by mass killing mobs with an AOE and selling the materials (mats) to the NPC stores. The good AOE skills unlock at level 70, and if you need boosts on those you need to be level 150+ If you don't want money and want to enjoy the game and the story normally while defeating mobs with hard work, you can do that. However, don't expect us to help you. That is because we already have a helping system in place by the name of mercenaries. Using「MENU」➡「Community」➡「Guild」➡「Mercinaries」or just simply friend mercenaries or even hired mercenaries, you can easily fight bosses. Furthermore, if a boss is hard, you need to exit (standing on exit for 10 seconds) and reenter in easy mode. If you want to do story with hard work and the feeling of winning, you need to do it on your own (or get a new player like you alongside) Iv'e already told you why people want to level up to level 150, it's to get stronger to fight mobs to earn spina. Do you Want to level up to 150 fast?
If you are a newbie, the fastest way to get to level 150 is to play story up to chapter 9 complete. You'll be at level 150 at the end of it. People will tell you to do the next chapters too. No need to do that, level 150 is enough. Save it up for future when you need exp to reach a benchmark level like 205 or 200.
Skills
Toram has a classless system meaning you can get skills from any skill tree. The weapon you choose in Tutorial only determines which skill page you unlock first.
To upgrade skills you need to get skill pages. Those can be bought at the Sofya City library. Some pages are in books that you can't find in the library. Those books have special skills. Those can be purchased from other players or be won as prizes from treasure chests. (Complete chapter 2 lesson 1 to understand what those are.)'
Skill pages are divide into tiers. Tier 0 to 4.
Most (NOT ALL) main weapon skill pages from tier 0 and 1 are unlocked at level 0
Tier 2 pages unlock at level 30
Tier 3 pages unlock at level 70
Tier 4 pages unlock at level 150
Those levels are the levels of your highest leveled character. If you have a level 120 character and a level 40 character, then even the level 40 character can use tier 3 skills. If you then delete the level 120 character, then whatever pages you had purchased for the level 40 character remain. But you cannot get new pages. Tier 4 skills are the strongest and help you level your other characters faster and also farm faster. Hence everyone keeps telling you to do story up to chapter 9 and to get to level 150. If you want, you can enjoy the game at your own pace (It helps you learn combat skills and mechanics. I recommend it) But you won't find many people around your level because, Toram is an old game. People are already high level.
Trading and Economy
You can trade with other people by tapping on them and clicking trading. You don't need to actually give them anything in trade, or even coins, though. You need to be able to see them though. As always, more options show up on list when you tap the top arrow. You can gift a friend, party member or a guild member from long distance. This is however only one item at a time. You can also mail them over long distances, so they can see it when they come back online. Sofya City, El Scaro and Hora Diomedea have Trading boards called consignment boards. You can register your item there, and people will search for your item and buy it. However, the government taxes you. You can even do cross - server in this method, but then there's an ADDITIONAL tax.
